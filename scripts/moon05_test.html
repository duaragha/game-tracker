Sand Kingdom Power Moon 05 - Overlooking the Desert Town

By Brendan Graeber,Sam Stewart,Miranda Sanchez,+1.1k more
Updated: Mar 28, 2025 8:05pm UTC
View Interactive Map

The Sand Kingdom Power Moon 05 - Overlooking the Desert Town is one of the Power Moons in the Sand Kingdom.

You can find this Power Moon in Quadrant D3, in the middle of Tostarena Town, both when the town is frozen and thawed.

To find it, cross the plaza and go left from the fountain to find a flower on the side of the building you can ground pound to jump all the way up to the rooftops. Head across the rooftops until you find a large blue and yellow tower where the Power Moon rests.
