Sand Kingdom Power Moons

By Brendan Graeber,Sam Stewart,Miranda Sanchez,+1.1k more
Updated: Apr 12, 2023 4:44am UTC

On this page you will find the guide for all Sand Kingdom Power Moons, with the locations and positions of every Power Moon that you can find in this region.

Power Moons are collectible objects that are needed to power up the Odyssey to travel to new destinations. Upon completing the main story, gaining additional Power Moons will allow you to unlock new Kingdoms and Costumes.

Below you will find the list for every Power Moon in Sand Kingdom. Please note that when first entering a Kingdom, there will be some Power Moons that are not currently obtainable, even if their number is listed. Certain Power Moons can only be obtained after completing all main objectives in a Kingdom - and even more will only appear after the main story is complete.

There are 89 total Power Moons and 100 Purple Coins in the Sand Kingdom. Click the map below to see their locations.

In addition to this, during Post Game there is a new ability to reveal even more Power Moons that will appear all over the map - these will be listed below the initial list of Power Moons. Click on a Power Moon to learn more about how to find it.

Want to collect all the Power Moons in Sand Kingdom? Keep this guide open, and use our interactive checklists below to mark off which Power Moons you have found.
